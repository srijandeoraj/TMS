Sentiment Analysis and Stock Price Prediction: An Investigation of a Tweet-Based Dataset

Michelle Zhu, Tanusri Mandapati, Srijan Deoraj

Home

Analysis on stock market movements has become a popular area of investigation, and despite prior beliefs, public opinion has been proven to have an impact on the movement of the stock market. In this paper, we apply sentiment analysis to a tweet-based dataset to investigate the how public sentiment can be used to predict stock market movements. Using a Naive Bayes Classifier and a linear regression model, we predicted the following day's opening stock price.
